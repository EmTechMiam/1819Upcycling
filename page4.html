Materials that can be Made Out of Used Papers, Magazines, or Newspapers

A. Necklace

1. Cut small strips of paper, magazine or newspaper.

2. Through a small stick, roll the strips of papers.

3. You now formed beads. 

4. Connect the beads through a string. 

5. Make sure that the necklace you're making is fit to the neck. 

6. If it's too fit to the neck, adjust it by adding more beads.

7. You  now have your necklace made from paper.

8. If you want, you can put varnish or paint to the necklace so that it will last longer and for a better look.

B. Bracelet

1. Cut strips of papers, magazines, or newspapers. 

2. Roll it to form beads. 

3. Connect the formed beads through a string.

4. You now formed a bracelet. You can varnish or paint it if you want.
