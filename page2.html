Useful Materials From Plastic Bottles

A. Ballpen/ Pencil Holder

1. Prepare the materials needed; scissors, glue, plastic bottle, colored paper. 

2. Cut the plastic bottle.

3. Make sure that the cut end of the plastic bottle is not sharp. 

4. Cover the bottle with a colored paper through the use of a glue.

5. Cut the ends of the colored paper if it's long enough for the bottle. 

6. Make rolled papers, magazines, or newspapers to serve as decoration for the  covered bottle.

7. Paste the rolled papers in the covered bottle through a glue.

8. You now have a ballpen/ pencil holder made out of plastic bottles.

B. Food Holder/ Container

1. Get two plastic bottles and cut it.

2. Take only the bottom part and make sure that they will fit together. 

3. From the left parts of the bottle, make a leaf out of it.

4. Cut a part from the bottle and roll it forming a stem.

5. Connect the leaf and the stem through a glue gun.

6. Glue gun the stem and leaf to the cut bottom part of the bottles.

7. Make sure that when you pull the handle (stem), it will not be detached from the cover.

8. You can now place a food in your food holder or container.
