RA 9003 or The Ecological Solid Waste Management Act

President Gloria Macapagal Arroyo signed the Republic Act No. 9003 into law on January 26, 2001.
RA 9003 is seen to be comprehensive enough in taking action on solid wastes.
The paradigm of RA 9003 is "waste is resource that can be recovered," emphasizing recycling, reuse and composting as methods to minimize and eventually manage waste problem
