Upcycling of Plastic Bottles, Juice Packs, and Used Papers, Magazines and Newspapers

This website aims to: show ways on how to turn plastic bottles, juice packs, and papers, magazines and newspapers into usable materials instead of throwing it anywhere; and enlighten the minds and influence the youth to join community projects in their place that promotes upcycling or recycling.

"We dream of youths who passionately love their environment and whose values and competencies enabe them to realize their full ability and contribute in helping and saving Mother Earth."
